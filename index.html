resume

During my career in the automotive industry, I had the privilege of working with several renowned automobile companies that shaped my passion for vehicles and innovation. It all started when I joined **Ford Motors**, where I learned the intricacies of manufacturing processes and the importance of reliability in engineering. Being part of a company with such a rich legacy gave me insight into the classic side of car design, and I gained a strong foundation in vehicle mechanics. From there, I moved to **Tesla**, eager to dive into the future of electric vehicles. This was a completely different experience, where I was involved in cutting-edge technology, sustainable design, and the development of electric powertrains. Working with Tesla's fast-paced environment taught me to think outside the box and embrace bold ideas. Later, I transitioned to **BMW**, known for its luxury and precision. Here, I got to focus on high-performance engineering and how luxury can blend with technology. My role here allowed me to work with premium materials and cutting-edge driving technology, which elevated my understanding of design excellence. Finally, I spent time with **Toyota**, where I was involved in their hybrid vehicle development and admired the company's dedication to efficiency, sustainability, and innovation. The emphasis on quality and long-term value made Toyota stand out as a leader in responsible engineering. Each of these companies left a unique mark on my career, shaping me into a versatile professional with a diverse understanding of the automotive world.
